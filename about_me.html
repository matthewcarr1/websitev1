About Me

Enthralled by the world of climbing, my journey into the outdoors commenced—a romance that blossomed further during my college years. This odyssey led me across continents, crafting adventures etched deeply into my soul.

Amidst these adventures, photography found me—a medium to share the narratives born in the wild. Whether suspending myself on ropes to seize climbers defying gravity, capturing poignant hikes, or immortalizing unguarded moments of joy, my lens binds emotions to pixels.

Now based in Indianapolis, I chase vertical triumphs worldwide. Candid and unfiltered, my photography encapsulates the genuine essence of each fleeting second, celebrating the authentic beauty in unscripted life.
