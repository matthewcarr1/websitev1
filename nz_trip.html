New Zealand November 2023

Trip Summary

In November of 2023, I went to go visit my friend Will in Christchurch, New Zealand. He was doing a post doc degree there, and offered to let me come visit. We started to plan the trip, which was going to be around 3 weeks for me. Unfortunately, Will had to work so he was not able to join for the whole trip. We decided that we were going to hang out in Christchurch for a a couple of days, then do the famous Milford Track together at the end of my trip. The rest of the trip, I was going to spend solo traveling from hostal to hostal.
